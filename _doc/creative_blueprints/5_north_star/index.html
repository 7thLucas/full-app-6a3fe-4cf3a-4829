Verified Operation

Automated Product Post Published

A scheduled post for a store product is successfully delivered to a connected social media platform — one confirmed, real-world promotional act. Clicks, logins, and previews are not operations.

WVO Formula

Posts per day: Assumption — adjust: 1 post/day
Platforms connected: Assumption — adjust: 5 platforms
Days per week: 7 days

1 post/day × 5 platforms × 7 days

= 35 posts / week

Value Derivation

Manual time/post: Assumption — adjust: 10 min
35 posts × 10 min: 350 min/wk (5.8 hrs)
Hourly rate: Assumption — adjust: $25/hr

5.8 hrs × $25/hr

≈ $145 saved / week

$145/wk × 52 weeks

≈ $7,540 / year
