The Challenge & The Fix

Why This Exists

✕ The Problem

Manual posting is a daily grind you can't sustain

Posting to Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, Pinterest, LinkedIn, and Twitter every single day takes 30–60 minutes. Most store owners quit within weeks — and inconsistent posting tanks algorithmic reach.

  • → Hours lost to repetitive daily posting tasks
  • → Missed days suppress platform reach and visibility
  • → Products go unpromoted for days or weeks at a time
✓ The Solution

Set it once. Your store promotes itself forever.

Upload your catalog, connect your social accounts, pick a daily time. The app rotates through your products and publishes automatically — on time, every time, to every platform.

  • → One-time setup produces perpetual daily output
  • → Every product gets promoted, automatically on rotation
  • → 6 major platforms covered from a single dashboard
