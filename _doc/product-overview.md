# Product Overview

## What It Is
An automated social media scheduling and product-promotion app for store owners. Users upload their product catalog, connect their social media accounts, and set a daily posting time — the app then rotates through the products and publishes to every connected platform automatically, every day, without further manual input. A true "set it and forget it" promotional engine.

## Problem It Solves
Store owners with digital or physical products need consistent social media presence to drive traffic and sales. Manually posting to five or six platforms every single day takes 30–60 minutes, is easy to skip, and nearly impossible to sustain long-term. Inconsistent posting suppresses algorithmic reach, leaves products invisible, and translates directly into lost revenue.

## Solution
Upload the catalog once, connect the social accounts once, set a daily posting time — then the app takes over. Products rotate automatically; one post goes out every day at the scheduled time across every connected platform. No agency, no daily effort, no missed days.

## Primary Users
- Solo store owners (digital products: ebooks, courses, templates, downloads; or physical products)
- Digital product creators who need consistent, broad promotional reach
- Small e-commerce businesses without a dedicated social media team

## Core Features
1. **Product Catalog Management** — add and manage products with name, image, description, price, and purchase link
2. **Multi-Platform OAuth Connections** — Instagram, Facebook, Twitter/X, TikTok, Pinterest, LinkedIn
3. **Rotation Scheduler** — configure a daily posting time; the app auto-selects the next product in the queue and publishes on schedule
4. **Automated Post Publishing** — posts go live at the configured time every day without user intervention
5. **Post Preview** — preview caption and image layout before any post publishes
6. **Posting History & Logs** — full record of every post: product, platform, timestamp, and delivery status

## Positioning
Premium but accessible. Built for non-technical store owners who want professional-grade, consistent social media promotion without hiring an agency or learning complex marketing tools.

## Brand Tone
Calm, empowering, confident. "You built the store — we run the daily promotion." The relief of marketing on autopilot rather than the hustle of manual daily posting.

## Scope (MVP)
- Product catalog with manual entry (name, image, description, price, link)
- OAuth connections for Instagram, Facebook, Twitter/X, TikTok, Pinterest, LinkedIn
- Daily rotation scheduler with user-configurable posting time
- Automated publishing engine (posts go out at the set time every day)
- Post preview before publish
- Posting history and status log

## Strategic Principles
- **Simplicity first**: complete setup in under 10 minutes; no marketing expertise required
- **Consistency as the core value**: one-time setup → perpetual daily output across all platforms
- **Broad platform reach**: maximum platform coverage from a single dashboard
